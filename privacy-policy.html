Introduction

Welcome to Lofa Academy. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information provided by students, parents, and staff within our community.

Data We Collect

We collect information that helps us provide a safe and effective learning environment, including:

  • Personal Identification: Name, age, and gender.
  • Contact Details: Address, phone numbers, and parent/guardian email addresses.
  • Academic Records: Grades, attendance, and disciplinary history.

How We Use Your Data

Information collected is strictly utilized for educational and administrative purposes, such as processing admissions, generating reports for the Ministry of Education, and maintaining student safety.

Student Privacy & Protection

In accordance with educational standards, we never share student data with third-party advertisers. Access to records is limited to authorized administrative staff and legal guardians.

Contact Our Privacy Officer

If you have questions about your data, please contact the Administration Office.
