The Welcome

Dear Families and Community Members, it is my distinct honor to welcome you to the current academic year. At our school, we believe that education is a partnership. My door is always open as we work together to ensure that every student feels safe, valued, and inspired to reach their full potential.

The Experience

With over 15 years in the field of education, my journey began in a 4th-grade classroom where I discovered my passion for literacy development. Before taking the helm here, I served as a district curriculum coordinator, focusing on specialized pedagogical credentials that bridge the gap between traditional and digital learning.

The Personal Touch

When I am not at the school, I am a dedicated parent of two. You can often find us exploring the local library or working in our garden. These moments at home remind me daily why the work we do here is so vital: we are raising the next generation of thinkers, doers, and kind-hearted citizens.

Mohammed Kamara

Principal, Zawordamai Elementary, Junior High School
