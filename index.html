Who We Are

Our Core Values

Built on a foundation of tradition and excellence, our values guide every lesson taught and every dream nurtured at Zawordamai.

Academic Excellence

We hold every student to a high standard, providing rigorous, structured learning from Kindergarten through Grade 9.

Community & Unity

We are rooted in the Lofa County community. Families, teachers, and students thrive together as one team.

Integrity & Discipline

Character is built through consistency. We teach students to be honest, responsible, and resilient in all they do.

Future Readiness

Beyond textbooks, we prepare students with critical thinking and life skills to succeed in secondary school and beyond.
